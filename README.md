# VidFlow AI – User Edition (Creators) V3.0-Multi

Welcome to **VidFlow AI**, the ultimate AI-powered YouTube content automation platform designed specifically for solo creators and beginners. User Edition V3.0-Multi now includes **Advanced AI** support.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start Instructions

1. **Set Up Your Environment**: Ensure you have a valid Google Gemini API Key.
2. **Launch the App**: Open the application URL in your browser.
3. **Create a Project**: Click on "New Project" in the Dashboard.
4. **Define Your Niche**: Enter your content niche and target audience.

---

## 📖 How to Use

VidFlow follows a logical 6-step workflow to take you from a blank page to a viral video package:

### Step 1: Idea Lab
- Enter your niche and audience.
- Select your Video Type (e.g., Education, News, Gaming).
- Click **Generate 10 Viral Ideas**.
- Click on an idea card to select it.

### Step 2: Script Studio
- Choose your **Script Mode** (Long-form or Short-form).
- Select a **Tone** (Informative, Storytelling, or Viral).
- Click **Generate Script**.
- Edit or copy the generated structured script.

### Step 3: SEO Optimizer
- Click **Generate SEO Metadata**.
- Get high-CTR titles, optimized descriptions, and relevant tags/hashtags.

### Step 4: Thumbnail Lab
- Click **Generate Thumbnail Concept**.
- Receive a detailed visual prompt that you can use in AI image generators like Midjourney or DALL-E.

### Step 5: Social Captions
- Click **Generate Social Captions**.
- Get tailored promotional content for Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), and LinkedIn.

### Step 6: Final Review & Export
- Review your entire content package.
- Use the **Copy All** or **Download Package** buttons to save your work.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **AI Generation Fails** | Check your internet connection and ensure your Gemini API Key is correctly configured in the environment. |
| **Ideas Not Loading** | Ensure you have filled out both the "Niche" and "Target Audience" fields. |
| **Script Not Generating** | Make sure you have selected an idea from the Idea Lab first. |
| **Styles Look Broken** | Refresh the page. VidFlow is optimized for modern browsers like Chrome, Edge, and Safari. |
